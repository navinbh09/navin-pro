Dye-Sensitized Solar Cell Research

Exploring sustainable and cost-effective solar energy solutions using natural dyes.

Project Overview

Solar energy is one of our best options for sustainable power, but traditional solar panels can be expensive and environmentally costly to produce. My research focuses on a promising alternative: dye-sensitized solar cells (DSSCs) that use natural dyes from plants to capture sunlight.

What Makes This Special?

  • Uses eco-friendly dyes from plants like beetroot and sumac
  • Much cheaper to produce than traditional solar cells
  • Can work well even in low-light conditions
  • Helps move us toward more sustainable energy solutions

Technical Highlights

  • Conducted detailed analysis using Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS)
  • Studied how different natural dyes affect solar cell performance
  • Optimized the cell design for better power conversion
  • Documented comprehensive findings in a research paper

Impact

  • Contributes to making solar energy more accessible
  • Supports the development of eco-friendly energy solutions
  • Helps advance understanding of natural dye applications
  • Creates opportunities for low-cost solar technology

Acknowledgment

I gratefully acknowledge the mentorship and guidance of Dr. Rasanjali Jayathissa during this research, conducted at Truman University University.

Smart Humidifier Control System

Automated climate control system using Raspberry Pi and smart sensors.

Project Overview

Created an intelligent humidifier control system that automatically maintains optimal humidity levels. This project combines hardware automation with smart monitoring to create a responsive and efficient climate control solution.

Technical Implementation

  • Raspberry Pi as the main control unit
  • Temperature and humidity sensors for real-time monitoring
  • 5V relay for controlling the humidifier
  • Custom Python code for automation logic

Key Features

  • Real-time humidity and temperature monitoring
  • Automated control based on sensor readings
  • Custom thresholds and scheduling options
  • Data logging for environment tracking

Applications

  • Home climate control automation
  • Plant growth environments
  • Laboratory humidity management
  • Comfortable living spaces maintenance
